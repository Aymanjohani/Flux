# HubSpot Custom Properties Setup Guide

## Overview
These 10 custom properties will help organize and score your manufacturing prospects.

## How to Create (HubSpot UI)

1. Go to **Settings** (⚙️ icon, top right)
2. Click **Properties** (left sidebar, under Data Management)
3. Click **Contact properties**
4. Click **Create property** button
5. Fill in details from tables below
6. Click **Create**

---

## Properties to Create

### 1. Manufacturing Subsector
- **Label:** Manufacturing Subsector
- **Internal name:** manufacturing_subsector
- **Type:** Dropdown select
- **Group:** Contact Information
- **Options:**
  - Food & Beverage
  - Chemicals & Petrochemicals
  - Automotive
  - Pharmaceutical
  - Plastics & Packaging
  - Steel & Metals
  - Electronics
  - Textiles
  - Building Materials
  - Oil & Gas Equipment
  - Water Treatment
  - Other Manufacturing

### 2. Geographic Region
- **Label:** Geographic Region
- **Internal name:** geographic_region
- **Type:** Dropdown select
- **Group:** Contact Information
- **Options:**
  - Jeddah
  - Riyadh
  - Dammam / Eastern Province
  - Jubail Industrial City
  - Yanbu Industrial City

### 3. Lead Temperature
- **Label:** Lead Temperature
- **Internal name:** lead_temperature
- **Type:** Dropdown select
- **Group:** Contact Information
- **Options:**

### 4. IIoT Lead Score
- **Label:** IIoT Lead Score
- **Internal name:** lead_score_iiot
- **Type:** Number
- **Group:** Contact Information
- **Description:** Automated lead score (0-100) based on engagement and fit
